TOP 5 SEINEN MANGA TO READ

Beserk

"Berserk" is a dark fantasy manga created by Kentaro Miura. The story follows Guts, a lone mercenary with a tragic past, as he battles through a world plagued by demons, corrupt knights, and eldritch horrors. Guts wields a massive sword, Dragonslayer, and seeks revenge against Griffith, a former ally turned antagonist. The narrative explores themes of fate, survival, and the consequences of one's choices. Known for its intricate plot, intense violence, and detailed artwork, "Berserk" delves into the human psyche and the struggle between light and darkness. The series has gained acclaim for its mature themes and is considered a classic in the manga genre.

Author - Kentaro Miura

Duration - August 1989 ~ Ongoing

Score - 9.47

Vagabond

"Vagabond" is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Takehiko Inoue. It is a fictionalized account of the life of the legendary samurai Miyamoto Musashi. The story follows Musashi's journey of self-discovery and mastery in the way of the sword during Japan's tumultuous Warring States period. Filled with intense sword fights, philosophical reflections, and intricate character development, "Vagabond" explores themes of honor, ambition, and the pursuit of true strength. The manga is renowned for its stunning artwork, dynamic action sequences, and deep exploration of the human spirit. Musashi's quest for enlightenment and martial prowess forms the heart of this compelling historical epic.

Author - Takehiko Inoue

Duration - Sep 1998 ~ May 2015

Score - 9.25

Monster

"Monster" is a Japanese manga written and illustrated by Naoki Urasawa. The gripping psychological thriller follows Dr. Kenzo Tenma, a brilliant neurosurgeon who faces moral dilemmas after saving the life of a young boy over a mayor. When the boy grows up to be a sociopathic killer, Dr. Tenma embarks on a quest to stop the monster he inadvertently created. The narrative unfolds with intricate plot twists, exploring themes of morality, justice, and the consequences of one's choices. Urasawa's masterful storytelling and complex characters make "Monster" a thought-provoking and suspenseful manga, captivating readers with its exploration of the human psyche.

Author - Naoki Urasawa

Duration - Dec 1994 ~ Dec 2001

Score - 9.15

Vinland Saga

Vinland Saga is a historical manga series by Makoto Yukimura. Set in the Viking Age, it follows Thorfinn, the son of a legendary warrior, seeking revenge for his father's murder. The narrative explores themes of war, honor, and the pursuit of a peaceful land called Vinland. Thorfinn's journey unfolds against a backdrop of historical events, showcasing intricate character development, intense battles, and political intrigue. The series skillfully blends fiction with real historical figures and events, providing a captivating and visceral portrayal of Norse culture and the tumultuous times in which it is set.

Author - Makoto Yukimura

Duration - April 2005 ~ Ongoing

Score - 9.06

Goodnight Punpun

"Goodnight Punpun" is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Inio Asano. The story follows Punpun Punyama, an emotionally troubled boy navigating the complexities of adolescence and adulthood. Depicting his life from elementary school to adulthood, the series explores themes of love, family, mental health, and existentialism.
